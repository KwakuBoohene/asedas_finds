Why
Though walking through racks of clothes is fun for some, it does get stressful looking for the perfect colour, size or style. This gets even worse for those who want absolutely nothing to do with clothing stores. Whether it’s limited time, no interest or proximity that hinders you from shopping in-person, AF is the best stop for you!
What
Aseda’s Finds is an online clothing shop for ladies’ clothes and other wardrobe pieces. AF prioritizes convenience, affordability, practicality, and fun to give you a stress-free shopping experience from the comfort of your home/office/seat. Indeed, there’s something for everyone and for every occasion.
How
With the clothing resale service and trusted local vendors, practical clothes are sourced with a careful selection process. When the clothes are uploaded to Instagram, Facebook, and this website, all the necessary information on each item is provided to make shopping easy. And then there’s the fun bit – we occasionally have engaging content to add some spice!
